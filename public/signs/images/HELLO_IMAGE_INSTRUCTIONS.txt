HELLO Sign Language Image Instructions
=====================================

Create a PNG image file named "hello.png" and place it in: public/signs/images/hello.png

Image Description:
- Top: The word "HELLO" in bold black letters
- Below: Five hands arranged horizontally showing ASL letters:
  1. H: Index and middle fingers extended together, thumb tucked
  2. E: All fingers curled toward palm, thumb on palm  
  3. L: Thumb and index finger extended upward, other fingers curled
  4. L: Same as above (second L)
  5. O: All fingers and thumb curled to form a circle

Image specifications:
- Format: PNG
- Dimensions: 400x300 pixels
- Background: White
- Hands: Black outline on white background
- Text: Bold black sans-serif font

The SignTranslator component will automatically load this when users search for "hello"
